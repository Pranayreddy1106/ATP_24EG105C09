#   ATP  -  Week  2  (Deep  in  Data) !!

dis  foldr  is  for  learning  more  about  javascript  arrays  and  objects  and  how  to  manipulate  them . 
we do  many  projects  like  bank  analyzer  and  shopping  cart . 

---

##   Topics  Covered :

-   we learn  how  to  calculate  bank  transactions  using  high  level  methods  like  map  and  filter . 
-   we build  movie  recommendation  system  based  on  user  likes  and  dislikes . 
-   student  dashboards  are  mak  with  dynamic  data  from  the  objects . 
-   we deep  dive  into  shallow  copy  and  deep  copy  (very  confusing  things) . 
-   spread  and  rest  operators  are  learn  to  mak  code  shorter  and  cleaner . 

---

##   Key  Projects :

1.   bankTransactionAnalyzer.js :   find  the  income  and  expense  from  the  list  of  money  moves . 
2.   movieRecomendation.js :   suggest  best  movies  to  watch  today . 
3.   shoppingCart.js :   add  and  remove  items  and  calculate  the  final  bill  amount . 
4.   employeePayroll.js :   calculate  salary  for  many  people  working  in  company .
